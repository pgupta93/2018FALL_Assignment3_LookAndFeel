What is Makeup? Makeup has all different types of meanings. To me personally makeup is art instead of drawing on paper you are applying that makeup on someones face and creating art on their face. Its a beautiful thing so makeup is “ART” Why is Makeup used? Makeup can serve a variety of reasons or purposes. Perhaps it makes one feel pretty. I know I feel glamorous with red lipstick.

It can help those who suffer from hyper pigmentation to even out their skin tone. It can camouflage acne and other facial maladies. It can make you appear softer or harder depending on your desires. Perhaps your cheekbones are not chiseled to your liking, you can redefine them by contouring. Or maybe they are too hard, you can soften them to your preference.

A woman with makeup on portrays herself as a person who takes care of herself and her looks. Makeup is basically made to accentuate your best features and conceal ones that make you feel insecure. So when you appear prettier, you draw attention and develop your self confidence. When you are confident, you become productive, assertive and contribute more in terms of actions and opinions, yes? What are the different types of Makeup? Cosmetics are just as diversified as the individuals who wear them. There are heavily pigmented cosmetics, for example, foundations, that are designed to give medium to full coverage, as well as sheer cover makeup for those that prefer a more natural look. The choices don't end there!
